FAQ

Common questions

Each page is focused on one client need, from filing taxes to uploading documents securely.

Can you help if my books are behind?

Yes. We can review bank statements, clean up categories, reconcile accounts, and prepare year-end reports before filing.

Can you help me start a company?

Yes. We can help with business formation planning and setup, then keep your tax, bookkeeping, and payroll questions organized from the start.

Do you offer HR consulting?

Yes. Our team can advise on recruiting, employment practices, compliance basics, and visa-related employment questions.

Do you offer Mandarin-English support?

Yes. Mandarin-English bilingual support is available for clients who prefer service in either language.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Bring last year’s return, current tax forms, business income and expense records, IDs, and any IRS or state notices.
